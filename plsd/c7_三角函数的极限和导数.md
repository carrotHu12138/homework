$$
\begin{split}
\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\sin(x)}{x}&=1. \\
\lim_{x \to 0}\cos(x)&=1. \\
\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\tan(x)}{x}&=\lim_{x \to 0}\cfrac{\cfrac{\sin(x)}{\cos(s)}}{x}=\lim_{x \to 0}\left(\frac{\sin(x)}{x}\right)\left(\frac{1}{\cos(x)}\right) = (1)\left(\frac{1}{1}\right) = 1\\
\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\tan(x)}{x}&=1
\end{split}

$$
